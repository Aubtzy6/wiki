City

City
Length 164 Studs
Cliffs 4
Date Added Feburary 16th, 2023
Built By coreyhsGames

City is medium map with city buildings. The map has trees and is similar to the Classic Roblox Game, Roblox HQ. The top of the buildings can be used to place cliff towers.


Trivia

  • City is based of both the City map from Tower Battles and the Classic Roblox Game, Roblox HQ.
  • City is the one of the first maps added in Beta.
