Sharpshooter

Sharpshooter
Tix Cost 100
Level Required 0
Starting Cost $560
Damage High (6)
Damage Type Single
Range Far (26)
Placement Cliff

The Sharpshooter is a single target tower. At Level 1, the Sharpshooter fires every 3.2 seconds, with a range of 26 and dealing 6 damage per shot (1.88 DPS). It costs 100 Tix and requires no level to purchase.


Appearance

The Sharpshooter appears as a green tower, wielding a M110 SASS.


Upgrades

Level 2 - Gloves

Cost: $310

  • Range increased from 26 to 30. (+4)
  • Damage increased from 6 to 8. (+2)
  • Cooldown decreased from 3.2s to 3s. (-0.2s)
  • DPS increased from 1.88 to 2.67. (+0.79)
  • Sharpshooter now wears gloves.

Level 3 - Radio

Cost: $500

  • Range increased from 30 to 32. (+2)
  • Damage increased from 8 to 10. (+2)
  • DPS increased from 2.67 to 3.33. (+0.66)
  • Sharpshooter now has hidden detection.
  • Sharpshooter now wears a headset.

Level 4 - Accurate Aim

Cost: $1500

  • Range increased from 32 to 35. (+3)
  • Damage increased from 10 to 16. (+6)
  • Cooldown decreased from 3s to 2.7s. (-0.3s)
  • DPS increased from 3.33 to 5.93. (+2.6)
  • Sharpshooter now wears a hat and the M110 SASS is darker in colour.

Level 5 - Bloxy Cola

Cost: $4000

  • Range increased from 35 to 38. (+3)
  • Damage increased from 16 to 45. (+29)
  • Cooldown decreased from 2.7s to 2.5s. (-0.2s)
  • DPS increased from 5.93 to 18. (+23.93)
  • Sharpshooter now wears sleeves and holds a Bloxy Cola.

Trivia

  • The Sharpshooter was the first ever cliff tower to be made in Retro Defense.
