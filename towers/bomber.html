Bomber

Bomber
Tix Cost 300
Level Required 0
Starting Cost $400
Damage Medium (4)
Damage Type Splash
Range Small (7)
Placement Ground

The Bomber is a splash target tower. At Level 1, the Bomber throws a bomb every 3.5 seconds, with a range of 7 and dealing 4 damage to each enemy in its explosion (1.14 DPS). It costs 300 Tix and requires no level to purchase.


Appearance

The Bomber appears as a gray tower, wielding an Roblox Classic Bomb.
