Bomber

Bomber
Unlock Cost 400 Tix
Level Required 0
Statistics
Base Cost $400
Base Damage 4
Base Range 8
Damage Type Splash
Placement Ground

The Bomber is a splash target tower. At Level 1, the Bomber throws a bomb every 3.5 seconds, with a range of 8 and dealing 4 damage to each enemy in its explosion (1.14 DPS). It costs 400 Tix and requires no level to purchase.


Appearance

The Bomber appears as a gray tower, wielding an Roblox Classic Bomb.

Trivia
