Recruit

Recruit
Unlock Cost Free
Level Required 0
Statistics
Base Cost $500
Base Damage 1
Base Range 12
Damage Type Single
Placement Ground

The Recruit is a single target tower. At Level 1, the Recruit fires every 0.23 seconds, with a range of 12 and dealing 1 damage per shot (4.35 DPS). It costs 0 Tix and requires no level to purchase.


Appearance

The Recruit appears as a red tower, wielding an AK-47.


Upgrades

Level 2 - Glasses

Cost: $275

  • Range increased from 12 to 14. (+2)
  • Recruit now wears shades.

Level 3 - Radio

Cost: $450

  • Range increased from 14 to 15. (+1)
  • Recruit now has hidden detection.
  • Recruit now has a headset.

Level 4 - Better Equipment

Cost: $1700

  • Range increased from 15 to 17. (+2)
  • Damage increased from 1 to 2. (+1)
  • Cooldown decreased from 0.23s to 0.2s. (-0.03s)
  • DPS increased from 4.35 to 10. (+5.65)
  • Recruit now has a AK-12.

Level 5 - Rank Up

Cost: $3300

  • Range increased from 17 to 19. (+2)
  • Damage increased from 2 to 5. (+3)
  • DPS increased from 10 to 25. (+15)
  • Recruit wears gloves, a helmet, and sleeves.

Trivia

  • The Recruit was the first ever tower to be made in Retro Defense.
