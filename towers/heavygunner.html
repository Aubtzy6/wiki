Heavy Gunner

Heavy Gunner
Tix Cost 1000
Level Required 5
Starting Cost $1550
Damage Low (1)
Damage Type Single
Range Medium (20)
Placement Ground

The Heavy Gunner is a single target tower. At Level 1, the Heavy Gunner fires every 0.12 seconds, with a range of 20 and dealing 1 damage per shot (8.33 DPS). It costs 1000 Tix and requires 5 level to purchase.


Appearance

The Heavy Gunner appears as a red tower, wielding an M134 Vulcan Minigun.
