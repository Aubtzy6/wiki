Coder

Creates games and scripts to earn you money! Generates money.

Cost: 2000

This tower serves a purely economic role, generating income at set intervals during a game. While its concept has potential, its current implementation falls short. The game's duration is too brief for the tower to recoup the significant investment it demands, making it an inefficient choice. Additionally, the game can be completed just as easily without the need for its income-generating abilities, leaving little incentive to include it in a strategy. As it stands, this tower is more of a novelty than a practical option for serious gameplay.

Upgrades

The top path maximizes this tower's income-generating potential, offering the highest monetary return at a relatively affordable cost. Its efficiency in producing large amounts of income quickly makes it the premier choice for players looking to amass resources in the shortest time possible. This path is the go-to option for those prioritizing economic growth during gameplay.


The bottom path offers a mix of income generation and utility, allowing the tower to regenerate health for the base and, at the X-4 upgrade, provide a 10% discount to all towers within its range. However, this path struggles to justify its steep investment. The 10% discount is underwhelming and limited in scope, especially when compared to the financial benefits of simply placing another top-path Coder. Additionally, the health regeneration feature, while helpful in theory, is often unnecessary if the resources required to upgrade to a X-4 CODER are instead used to strengthen defenses. Ultimately, this path fails to compete with the more efficient and impactful top path, making it a suboptimal choice.
